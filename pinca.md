## Pinca (Croatian Easter Bread)

### Short Overview
Activate fresh yeast → mix enriched dough → knead until elastic → proof twice → shape, score cross → bake until golden.

---

### Ingredients

#### Dough
- 500 g all-purpose flour  
- 80 g sugar  
- 20 g fresh yeast  
- 200 ml whole milk (lukewarm, 30–35°C)  
- 80 g unsalted butter (softened)  
- 2 eggs (~100 g without shell)  
- 2 egg yolks (~35–40 g)  
- Zest of 1 lemon (~2 g)  
- Zest of 1 orange (~3 g)  
- 15 ml rum (optional)  
- 8 g vanilla sugar (or 5 ml vanilla extract)  
- 5 g salt  

#### Topping
- 1 egg (~50 g)  
- 20 g coarse sugar  
- 20–30 g sliced almonds (optional)  

---

### Instructions

1. **Activate yeast**  
   Crumble yeast into a bowl. Add 50 ml milk + 5 g sugar. Stir and rest 5–10 min until foamy.

2. **Mix dough**  
   Combine flour, remaining sugar, salt, zest, vanilla. Add eggs, yolks, butter, rum, yeast mixture, and remaining milk. Mix to form dough.

3. **Knead**  
   Knead 10–15 min until smooth, elastic, slightly tacky.

4. **First rise**  
   Cover and proof 90–120 min at ~24–26°C until doubled.

5. **Shape**  
   Deflate gently, shape into a tight round loaf. Place on baking tray.

6. **Second rise**  
   Proof 45–60 min until puffy.

7. **Finish**  
   Brush with beaten egg. Cut a deep cross on top. Sprinkle sugar and almonds.

8. **Bake**  
   Bake at 175°C for 30–40 min until golden brown.

9. **Cool**  
   Cool completely before slicing.

---

### Yield
- 1 loaf (~900–1,000 g)  
- 8–10 servings
