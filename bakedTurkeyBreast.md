# Quick Mix-and-Bake Turkey Breast (No Marination Time)

## Ingredients (2 portions)
- 800g g turkey breast slices  
- 3–4 tbsp olive oil  
- 2 cloves garlic, finely minced  
- 1 tsp sweet paprika  
- ½ tsp dried oregano or thyme  
- small amount of Salt and black pepper  
- a splash of lemon juice  

## Oven
- **Convection (fan):** 165 °C  
- **Top/Bottom heat:** 185 °C

## Preparation
1. Preheat the oven.  
2. Put the turkey breast slices directly into a bowl.  
3. Add olive oil, garlic, spices, salt, and pepper.  
4. Mix thoroughly with your hands so all pieces are evenly coated.  
5. Transfer immediately into a baking paper covered tray, spreading the slices out flat.  
6. **Cover** the dish with a lid or aluminum foil.  
7. Bake for **20 minutes**.  
8. Remove the cover and bake another **20 minutes** until lightly browned.  
9. Rest for 3 minutes before serving.

## Notes
- Covering the dish at the beginning is crucial for juiciness.  
- Do not exceed ~75 °C internal temperature.  
- For more color, finish with 2–3 minutes under the grill.
