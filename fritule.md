# Croatian Fritule (for 2 people)

## Ingredients
- 120 g all-purpose flour  
- 12 g sugar (1 tbsp)  
- 1/2 tsp baking powder  
- Pinch of salt  
- 1 egg  
- 120 ml plain yogurt (or milk)  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 1 tbsp rum (optional)  
- Zest of 1/2 lemon or orange (optional)  
- 1–2 tbsp raisins (optional)  
- Oil for frying  
- Powdered sugar for dusting  

## Instructions
1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.  
2. Add egg, yogurt (or milk), vanilla, and rum. Mix until you get a smooth, thick batter.  
3. Stir in citrus zest and raisins if using.  
4. Heat 2–3 cm of oil in a small pot to around 170–180°C.  
5. Drop small spoonfuls of batter into the oil and fry until golden (about 2–3 minutes).  
6. Remove fritule and drain on paper towels.  
7. Dust with powdered sugar and serve warm.
